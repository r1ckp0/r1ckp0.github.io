about me

I like to work with music and sound. Although in the last years I've been getting more into other forms of expression, I'm currently focused on performing as a violinist and on the project "Rickpo" in which I sing and produce, mixing genres of popular music like EDM, pop or hip-hop.

Things that inspire me: fantasy books, old consoles, sad manga, pokémon, objective realism of the last century and table tennis, among many other things. People who know me would probably define me as reserved, and it is the artistic practices in which I get involved my (only) means of expression, and they are what brings me happiness, what causes me frustration, what helps me in difficult moments and what connects me with more people.

Something that is always in my practices is what I have decided to call "sound okonomiyaki". Okonomiyaki is a Japanese dish and roughly translates to "the things you like fried". That's something I look for when I'm creating or performing: to celebrate my background and my influences and fry them (artistically).
